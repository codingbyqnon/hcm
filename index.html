ABOUT HCM

Holder Capital Markets ($HCM) is a decentralized social-financial experiment created on the Solana blockchain. Its ultimate mission is to establish ticker purity and market identity through the principle of Single Ticker Use (STU). This principle holds that only one token with a given ticker should exist, be bonded, and recognized as the official version. In an environment filled with duplicate tokens, $HCM responds to the issues of duplication and brand dilution. If Pump.fun introduced a registry-based system for authentic tickers and a community-driven validation model, this would change how meme tokens establish identity, trust, and long-term value.

HOW TO BUY

Create a Wallet

Set up a compatible wallet by downloading and installing a Solana-compatible wallet such as Phantom or Solflare.

Fund With SOL

Purchase Solana (SOL) on a major exchange (like Coinbase or Bitget) and transfer the tokens to your new web3 wallet address. You will need enough SOL to cover the cost of the tokens you want to buy and the small transaction fees.

Go to Pumpfun

connect to Pump.fun directly via your wallet. Once connected copy the contract address from above and paste in the search bar to find HCM.

Swap $SOL for $HCM

Enter the amount of SOL you would like to awap with and confirm the transaction. Congratulations you have purchased $HCM
